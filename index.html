About Me

Who am I?

I’m a 26 year old professional golfer from Huddersfield, England. I play out of Meltham GC and Woodsome Hall GC, both in West Yorkshire. I currently play on the UK based PGA Europro Tour.

How I started

I come from a family full of golf fanatics! My dad is a very accomplished player (playing off a handicap of 4), and both my grandads have been very keen golfers for a long time. Hence, I was playing with plastic clubs as soon as I could walk! I’ve been a member at my home club Meltham since the age of 7.

Why golf?

I had a go at every sport going when I was a kid, before settling on football and golf. At age 13, I quit football so that I could focus more on golf. I loved the feeling of a well struck golf shot, and I got hooked on the process of practicing, improving, and seeing the results out on the course.

My Ambitions

I managed to achieve many of the ambitions I had growing up, during my amateur career. I represented England, travelled the world and won national and international tournaments. But I have many goals still to achieve. I want to play and win on the European Tour, PGA Tour, win majors, and become the best player I can be.

Other Hobbies and Interests

I am starting to realize more and more the importance of having other interests outside of golf, to maintain a healthy, balanced lifestyle. My hobbies off the course include playing guitar, watching football (Arsenal if you were wondering!) and I have also recently started to learn computer coding. I actually coded this website myself!
